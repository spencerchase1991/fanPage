James Lance Bass

Lance is an American singer, dancer, actor, film and television producer, and author. He grew up in Mississippi and rose to fame as the bass singer for the American pop boy band NSYNC. NSYNC's success led Bass to work in film and television.

  • Born: May 4, 1979
  • Spouse: Michael Turchin, married 2014
  • Music Groups: NSYNC, Bravo All Stars
  • Books: Out of Sync
  • Movies: I now Pronounce you Chuck and Larry, On the Line, Love Wrecked, Hell and Back, Such Good People and more.
